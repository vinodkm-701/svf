Our Mission

At Sarvo Vikash Foundation, our mission is to weave a story of resilience and hope by transforming challenges into opportunities for the most underserved communities. We believe that true progress comes from empowering individuals to become changemakers within their own lives and communities. Rooted in our unique journey as a Bengali community in Karnataka, we are driven to not only address immediate needs but also to foster long-term, self-sustaining solutions. Our mission is to create an ecosystem where education sparks innovation, healthcare ensures dignity, empowerment fuels equality, and sustainable practices protect our planet for future generations. What sets us apart is our focus on inclusivity and cultural heritage, celebrating the strength of diverse backgrounds while uniting efforts to uplift all. By combining grassroots action with forward-thinking initiatives, we aim to build a legacy of transformation that inspires others to join us in shaping a better world.

About us

Sarvo Vikash Foundation is more than just an NGO—it's a movement rooted in the heart of Sindhanur, Karnataka, driven by a unique story of migration, resilience, and a deep commitment to upliftment. Our journey began with the migration of Bengali families from Bangladesh during the India-Pakistan war in the early 1971. Settling in four distinct villages of Sindhanur, we built a life of perseverance amidst challenges, carrying with us the spirit of unity, hope, and community.
